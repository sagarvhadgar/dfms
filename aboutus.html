Overview

Revolutionizing facility management

Dynamite Facility Management Services is a trusted name in integrated facility management, offering a full spectrum of services tailored to meet diverse business and residential needs.

Founded by veterans and industry leaders with over four decades of combined experience, we bring the perfect blend of military-grade discipline and corporate expertise to every project.

Our commitment lies in delivering high-quality, cost-effective, and sustainable solutions while building long-term partnerships based on trust, transparency, and performance. We are self-performing with no sub-contractors across Mumbai & Gujarat.

Mission

Our Mission

To provide environment friendly services, with the help of best practices to obtain efficiency, ease, efficacy and employee satisfaction.

Vision

Our Vision

To be a leader in the field of Facility Services to focus and convert non-core business activities in targeted segments and high growth with lifetime relationships with stakeholders.

What We Stand For

The DFMS Way

Our values guide every site we serve — from corporate towers to healthcare facilities across Mumbai & Gujarat.

Empowering Spaces

Every client facility is maintained as if it were our own — clean, fully operational, and welcoming. We deliver visual and hygienic standards that reflect the quality your organisation deserves.

Enabling Excellence

We are self-performing with no sub-contractors — giving us full control over quality, costs, and timelines. Military-grade discipline meets corporate expertise on every assignment.

Our People, Our Pride

The housemen, chamber maids, and field teams in DFMS uniforms are the real strength and face of our company. We attract, train, and retain skilled professionals who take genuine pride in their work.

Trust & Transparency

We build long-term partnerships based on trust, transparency, and performance. Our client-centric approach focuses on your needs first — delivering value over profit, every time.
